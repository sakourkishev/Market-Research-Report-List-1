<p><p>Belt markets encompass various types designed for specific mechanical applications. Flat belts are used for power transmission across long distances. Round belts are flexible and suitable for small pulleys. V belts have a trapezoidal cross-section for grip in small spaces. Multi-groove belts combine multiple V belts for increased power. Timing belts synchronize rotation with toothed designs. Ribbed belts enhance traction and efficiency. Other belts include specialty options for unique machinery needs, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
