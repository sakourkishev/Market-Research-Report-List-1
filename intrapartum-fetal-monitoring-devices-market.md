<p><p>Intrapartum fetal monitoring devices include several key types. Fetal scalp electrodes provide direct monitoring of the fetal heart rate by attaching to the fetal scalp. Intrauterine pressure catheters measure intrauterine pressure to assess uterine contractions accurately. Transducers for fetal heart rate (FHR) non-invasively detect the baby's heartbeat. Similarly, transducers for uterine contractions monitor contraction patterns. This market offers essential tools for healthcare providers to ensure maternal and fetal well-being during labor and delivery.</p></p>
