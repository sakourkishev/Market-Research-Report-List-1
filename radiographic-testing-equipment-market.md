<p><ul><li>Volumetric Examination</li><li>Surface Examination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiographic Testing Equipment encompasses two primary market types: Volumetric Examination and Surface Examination. Volumetric Examination utilizes techniques like X-ray and gamma-ray imaging to inspect the internal structure of materials, detecting flaws within. In contrast, Surface Examination focuses on analyzing the outer surfaces of components using methods such as ultrasonic testing or eddy current testing. Both approaches are essential for ensuring the integrity and quality of materials in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
