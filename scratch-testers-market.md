<p><p>Scratch testers evaluate material resistance to scratches and abrasions, essential in industries like coatings, plastics, and metals. The Ordinary Scratch Tester offers a basic measurement of scratch resistance using a fixed load. The Linear Scratch Tester provides more detailed assessments by simulating real-life scratching conditions along a linear path. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing simplicity, precision, and throughput in scratch resistance evaluation.</p></p>
