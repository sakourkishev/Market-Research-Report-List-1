<p><strong>The Cotton Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross Layer Type</li><li>Parallel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton nonwoven fabric market is primarily categorized into two types: cross layer and parallel type. Cross layer nonwoven fabrics are produced by interlacing fibers in multiple directions, enhancing strength and durability, making them suitable for applications like medical supplies and hygiene products. In contrast, parallel type nonwovens have fibers aligned in a single direction, providing a smooth texture and uniformity, ideal for applications such as disposable apparel and home textiles. Both types offer unique benefits depending on their intended use.</p></p>
