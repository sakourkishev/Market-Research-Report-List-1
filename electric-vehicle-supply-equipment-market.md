<p><p>Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) market encompasses two primary types of charging systems: AC charging and DC charging. AC charging involves supplying alternating current to the vehicle, often used for home or public charging stations, providing a slower but more cost-effective option suitable for overnight charging. In contrast, DC charging delivers direct current, allowing for rapid charging at commercial stations, significantly reducing charging time. Both types cater to varying user needs, reflecting the growing demand for efficient EV infrastructure.</p></p>
