<p><ul><li>Ordinary Flat Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li><li>Rolled Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The architecture flat glass market comprises several types, including ordinary flat glass, float glass, and rolled glass. Ordinary flat glass is commonly used for applications like windows and doors due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Float glass, produced by floating molten glass on molten tin, offers a smooth surface and high clarity, making it ideal for architectural and decorative applications. Rolled glass, produced by rolling molten glass into sheets, is often used for glazing and interior design due to its aesthetic appeal and versatility.</p></p>
