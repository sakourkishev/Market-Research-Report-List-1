<p><p>The waterproof flooring market primarily includes three-layer and multilayer systems. Three-layer flooring typically consists of a wear layer, core layer, and backing layer, offering durability and moisture resistance. It's ideal for residential use. In contrast, multilayer flooring features additional layers, enhancing stability, insulation, and sound absorption, making it suitable for both residential and commercial applications. Both types combine aesthetics with functionality, providing consumers with a variety of design options while ensuring protection against water damage.</p></p>
