<p><p>The medical polymeric microspheres market includes PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) and PLGA (Poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid)) microspheres, each serving distinct applications. PMMA microspheres are commonly utilized for drug delivery, diagnostic imaging, and as bone cement in orthopedic surgeries due to their biocompatibility and stability. In contrast, PLGA microspheres are highly favored for controlled drug release, tissue engineering, and vaccine delivery, owing to their biodegradable nature. Both types facilitate advancements in targeted therapies and enhance patient care solutions.</p></p>
