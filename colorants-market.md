Masterbatches are pre-mixed color formulations for plastics, enabling easy application. Color concentrates involve high pigment content mixtures for specific coloring needs. Additional categories may include fluorescent, phosphorescent, and specialty colorants, catering to diverse industry applications and enhancing product appeal.</p></p>
