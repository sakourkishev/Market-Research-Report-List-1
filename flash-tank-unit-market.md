<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flash tank units are categorized based on their operational characteristics. Vertical flash tanks are designed for high-pressure applications, allowing for efficient separation of vapor and liquid in a compact footprint. They are often used in steam generation systems. Level flash tanks, on the other hand, focus on maintaining a consistent liquid level to optimize vaporization, primarily in processes requiring precise control over fluid dynamics. Both types play crucial roles in thermal energy recovery and process optimization in various industries.</p></p>
