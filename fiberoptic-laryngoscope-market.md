<p><p>The Fiberoptic Laryngoscope market is divided into two primary types: rigid and flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes. Rigid fiberoptic laryngoscopes offer enhanced visualization and stability, making them suitable for specific surgical procedures and direct visualization of the larynx. In contrast, flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes provide greater maneuverability and are ideal for challenging anatomical pathways, allowing for non-invasive examinations. Both types are essential in ENT practices, facilitating accurate diagnoses and enhancing the performance of various medical procedures focused on the airway.</p></p>
