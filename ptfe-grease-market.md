<p><ul><li>Dispersion Method</li><li>Suspension Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE grease is primarily categorized into two types based on its manufacturing methods: dispersion method and suspension method. The dispersion method involves evenly distributing PTFE particles in a suitable lubricant, enhancing its lubricating properties and reducing friction. In contrast, the suspension method entails suspending PTFE particles in a thicker base oil, which provides a more viscous formulation, offering superior stability and protection. Both methods cater to various industrial applications, emphasizing durability and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
