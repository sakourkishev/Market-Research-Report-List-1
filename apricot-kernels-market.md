<p><p>The apricot kernels market is primarily divided into two types: bitter and sweet. Bitter apricot kernels contain amygdalin, which can release cyanide when metabolized, making them popular in alternative medicine and health products, despite safety concerns. Sweet apricot kernels, on the other hand, are used in culinary applications and as snacks due to their pleasant taste and lower toxicity. The demand for each type varies based on consumer preferences, health trends, and regulatory considerations surrounding their use.</p></p>
