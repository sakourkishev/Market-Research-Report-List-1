<p><p>The radiotherapy instrument market encompasses several advanced treatment technologies. Gamma Knife is used for precise targeting of brain tumors using focused gamma radiation. TOMO Therapy integrates CT scanning with radiation therapy for better accuracy. Proton Knife utilizes protons for targeted therapy, minimizing damage to surrounding tissue, while Heavy Ion Knife employs heavy ions for aggressive cancer treatment, offering distinctive advantages in precision and effectiveness.</p></p>
