<p><p>The veterinary infectious disease diagnostics market encompasses various types of testing methods, including immunodiagnostics and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). Immunodiagnostics involve the use of antibodies to detect pathogens in animals, aiding in the rapid diagnosis of infections. ELISA is a specific immunodiagnostic test that quantitatively measures proteins, such as antigens or antibodies, in biological samples. Both methods play a crucial role in identifying and managing infectious diseases in veterinary practice, enhancing animal health and welfare.</p></p>
