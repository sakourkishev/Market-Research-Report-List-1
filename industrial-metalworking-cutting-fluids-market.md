<p><p>The industrial metalworking cutting fluids market encompasses various types including neat oil, emulsion, semi-synthetic, and synthetic fluids. Neat oil cutting fluids consist of straight mineral oil, providing superior lubrication without dilution. Emulsion fluids combine oil and water, offering cooling and lubrication. Semi-synthetic fluids blend both oil and water for balanced performance, while synthetic fluids are fully formulated with chemical components, ensuring high cooling and lubrication efficiency. Each type caters to specific machining needs and operational conditions in metalworking processes.</p></p>
