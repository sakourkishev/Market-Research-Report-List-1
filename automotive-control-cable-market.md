<p><strong>The Automotive Control Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive control cable market is segmented into single core and multi-core cables, catering to different vehicle needs. Single core cables are designed for straightforward applications, offering simplicity and ease of installation, often used in basic systems. In contrast, multi-core cables consist of multiple conductors, allowing for more complex functions and enhanced control across various systems, such as lighting, instrumentation, and communication. The choice between these types depends on the vehicle's design requirements and functionalities, impacting performance and efficiency.</p></p>
