<p><p>The radio modem market encompasses various types of communication technologies. License-free frequency modems operate in ISM bands, allowing users to transmit without licenses for short-range applications. UHF modems offer longer range and better penetration through obstacles, making them suitable for industrial uses. Wi-Fi modems leverage high data rates for wireless internet access in urban settings. VHF modems provide longer-range communication with high reliability, often used in marine and aviation sectors. Each type serves unique needs based on range, frequency, and application.</p></p>
