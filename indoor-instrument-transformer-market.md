<p><strong>The Indoor Instrument Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Transformer</li><li>Potential Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor instrument transformer market comprises two primary types: Current Transformers (CTs) and Potential Transformers (PTs). Current Transformers are used to measure alternating current by producing a reduced current proportional to the input, enabling safe monitoring and protection in electrical systems. Potential Transformers, on the other hand, step down high voltages to lower levels for measurement and protection purposes. Both play crucial roles in ensuring accurate data collection, system efficiency, and safety in electrical power infrastructure.</p></p>
