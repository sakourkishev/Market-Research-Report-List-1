<p><p>The truck suspension system market includes various types designed for improved ride quality and handling. Air suspension utilizes air-filled bags to provide adjustable cushioning, enhancing comfort and load capacity. Hydraulic suspension employs liquid to absorb shocks, ensuring a smooth ride under varying loads. Electromagnetic suspension uses electromagnetic forces to adapt stiffness dynamically, offering advanced control over vehicle handling. Electro-hydraulic suspension combines electrical and hydraulic systems for precise movement and responsiveness, optimizing performance and stability. Each type serves specific needs in the trucking industry.</p></p>
