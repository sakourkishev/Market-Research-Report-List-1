<p><strong>The Groove Milling Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Grooving</li><li>Internal Grooving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The groove milling tools market is divided into external and internal grooving segments. External grooving tools are designed for creating grooves on the outer surfaces of workpieces, making them ideal for components requiring precise edge profiles. In contrast, internal grooving tools are used to machine grooves within the interior surfaces of parts, essential for applications like oil and coolant passages. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing machining efficiency and precision in part design and functionality.</p></p>
